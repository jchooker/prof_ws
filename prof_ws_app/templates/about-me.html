Hi. My name is Joseph Hooker. I am from the Memphis area. During childhood, I remember computers being a domain of nerd esoterica. 14.4 kbps dial-up, Netscape, Prodigy, etc etc. Things that happened behind the scenes in technology were opaque: neon-green alphanumerical runes ordered in lines on a black command-line terminal.

I began programming in 2004, with Java, HTML, and CSS. Much of what I found unapproachable about programming persisted. I did not continue in the field, earning a B.A. in Art from Hendrix College instead. But when I picked up coding again in 2019, a number of things had changed in the tech industry.

I am currently working on a Master's in Computer Science at the University of Arkansas - Little Rock, and have recently completed a Web Development program at the Coding Dojo bootcamp, which provided the hands-on experience in Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and so on, that I had so desired.

I live in the Little Rock area with my wife, Emily, and our dog, Butler. I am a freelance web developer and also work in print production.
